Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

1. Overview

ClipCraft is a desktop application operated by Kongregate ("we", "us", "our") that processes video content locally on your computer and uploads clips to social media platforms on your behalf. This policy explains what data ClipCraft accesses and how it is handled.

2. Data We Collect

ClipCraft does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All video processing happens locally on your machine. We do not operate any backend servers or databases that receive your information.

3. Third-Party Platform Access

ClipCraft connects to third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) through their official APIs using OAuth 2.0 authentication. When you authorize ClipCraft, the following occurs:

For TikTok: ClipCraft requests permission to upload and publish video content to your TikTok account. We access your account solely to post video clips you have chosen to upload. We do not read, collect, or store your TikTok profile data, messages, followers, or any other account information beyond what is required to complete the upload.

For YouTube: ClipCraft uses the YouTube Data API v3 to upload videos to your channel. We access your account solely to post video clips and set their metadata (title, description, tags, privacy status).

For Instagram: ClipCraft uses the Meta Graph API to publish Reels to your Instagram account. We access your account solely to post video content.

4. OAuth Tokens

Authentication tokens are stored locally in a configuration file on your computer. These tokens are never transmitted to any server operated by us. You can revoke ClipCraft's access at any time through each platform's app settings, and you can delete your local tokens by removing them from the configuration file.

5. Video Content

Your video files are processed entirely on your local machine. Video content is only transmitted directly from your computer to the destination platform (YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram) when you initiate an upload. We never have access to your video files.

6. Analytics and Tracking

ClipCraft does not include any analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, or usage monitoring. We do not track how you use the application.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties. The only data transmission that occurs is the direct upload of video content from your computer to the platform you have selected.

8. Data Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to retain. Your local configuration and tokens persist on your machine until you delete them.

9. Children's Privacy

ClipCraft is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
