Desktop Application for Content Creators

ClipCraft

Automatically create, caption, and distribute short-form video clips to multiple social media platforms from a single desktop application.

AI-Powered Clip Detection

Automatically identifies the best moments from long-form videos using AI scoring for virality, humor, and engagement potential.

Smart Captioning

GPU-accelerated transcription with automatic subtitle generation, styled and timed for maximum readability on mobile.

Multi-Platform Upload

Upload directly to YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook, and X with platform-specific formatting and scheduling.

Content Library

Browse, manage, and re-upload all generated clips. Track upload status per platform, retry failed uploads, and delete unwanted content.

Upload Scheduling

Intelligent per-platform upload gaps prevent spam detection. Configure posting frequency and let the system manage timing automatically.

VOD Processing

Process 8-hour VODs efficiently by extracting audio first, transcribing with Whisper, then downloading only the highlight segments.
